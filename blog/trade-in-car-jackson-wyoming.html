How to Get the Best Trade-In Value

A few things you can do before bringing your vehicle in:

  • Clean it — Sounds simple, but a detailed interior and washed exterior genuinely affects perceived value. It shows you maintained the vehicle.
  • Gather records — Oil changes, tire rotations, any major service. Documented maintenance history adds real dollars.
  • Fix the small stuff — Replace burnt-out bulbs, fix a cracked windshield if it's cheap, top off fluids. Small investments return more than they cost.
  • Know your vehicle — Look up your VIN on KBB or Edmunds so you have a baseline. I'll show you my numbers regardless, but it helps to come informed.
