Tundra Buyers — What to Know Before You Shop

A few things I tell every Tundra buyer in Jackson: get the 4WD. Teton Pass and Hoback Canyon in February demand it. The CrewMax cab is worth the extra cost if you have a family — the back seat is the biggest in the class. And if you tow, the TRD Off-Road or Limited trims come with the tow package standard.

Skip the two-wheel-drive models unless you're only driving on plowed highways. In Jackson Hole, that's not realistic.
