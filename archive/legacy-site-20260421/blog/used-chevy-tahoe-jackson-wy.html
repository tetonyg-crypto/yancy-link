What to Look For in a Used Tahoe

Not every used Chevy Tahoe in Jackson WY is a good buy. Here's what I check before putting one on our lot at Stone's Auto Group:

Transfer case condition. In a mountain town, the 4WD system gets used hard. I verify it shifts cleanly between 2HI, 4HI, and 4LO. The AFM (Active Fuel Management) system on the 5.3L V8 can cause lifter issues — I check for any tick or misfire. And I look at the undercarriage for rust, especially on trucks that came from salt-heavy states.

Tahoe vs. the Competition in Wyoming

People always ask me: Tahoe or Suburban? Tahoe or Yukon? Tahoe or Expedition? Here's my honest take — the Tahoe hits the sweet spot. The Suburban is great if you need the extra row, but it's a pain to park on the Town Square. The Yukon is the same truck with a different grille and a higher price tag. The Expedition rides smoother but Ford's reliability track record makes me nervous past 100K.

For a used Chevy Tahoe in Jackson, WY, the value proposition is hard to beat. It's the right size, the right capability, and the right price for mountain life.
