Experience

A career compounded across five chapters.

  1. 2019 — 2023 · Salesperson

    Automotive sales.

    Began my automotive sales career in 2019 at a Jackson, Wyoming dealership. Four years on the floor — walk-in to delivery, trade evaluation to F&I, bilingual end to end. Thousands of customer interactions taught me how retail actually works. This is where it started.

  2. 2022 — 2024 · Founder

    Yancy Regional Services

    Trucking company founded to move heavy-duty commercial equipment across the American Southwest. Ran RV transport lanes from Elkhart, Indiana — the RV capital of the country — down to dealerships in Arizona and New Mexico. Built the operational playbook from the ground up: sourcing loads, managing routes, handling driver logistics, and running a clean fleet. The company sold in 2023.

  3. 2024 — 2025 · Business Consultant

    RADM CRE

    Commercial real estate financial consulting practice serving dealership owners, multifamily operators, and medical aesthetics investors. Built the outbound engine that produced the pipeline — cold email infrastructure, funnel architecture, landing page design, and automation stacks. Five years in sales taught me how to close. RADM is where I learned how to build the machine that creates the opportunity in the first place.

  4. 2025 — 2026 · Operator, on the floor

    Back on the floor.

    Returned to sales at two Jackson, Wyoming dealerships — a Subaru store and a Chevy store. The goal was not to sell cars — it was to live-test the product I was building from the ground up. Every lead running through the CRM became a trial run of the execution layer between intake and close. The floor is where Brevmont got made real.

  5. BREVMONT
    Present · Founder

    Brevmont Labs

    An AI layer that lives inside the CRM. A new lead comes in — the text, email, and CRM note are already written. A deal stalls — it gets surfaced before it rots. The follow-up that never happens on a busy floor, happens here. Built by the guy who had to do it, not theorized in a conference room.
